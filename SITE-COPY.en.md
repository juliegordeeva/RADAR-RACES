**Section label:** About

**Title:**  
Learning through experience that changes thinking

**Paragraphs:**

1. RADAR RACES is an innovative approach to developing leadership skills. We combine the extreme experience of motorsport with a deep analysis of management competencies.
2. The Experiential Learning format lets participants step beyond the familiar, rethink their management style and gain new insights to become more effective as leaders.

## 03. Format

**Section label:** Format

**Title:**  
Two parts of one intensive

**Subtitle:**  
Learning through new experience: first — laps on a professional track, then — a debrief of conscious and unconscious reactions and work on leadership competencies.

**Parts:**

### Part 01

**Tag:** Part 1 · Track

**Title:** Racing laps and reaction debrief

**Description:** Participation in laps on a professional track followed by an analysis of conscious and unconscious reactions.

**List:**

- Experience of fast decision-making under stress
- Risk management and real-time situation assessment skills
- Understanding your reactions and behavioral patterns in extreme conditions
- Awareness of personal limits and growth potential
- Team interaction practice in an unconventional setting

### Part 02

**Tag:** Part 2 · Session

**Title:** Conscious self-management as a leader

**Description:** A session on the conscious management of personal and professional skills, talents and energy.

**List:**

- Deep understanding of strengths and growth areas as a leader
- Tools to boost personal and professional effectiveness
- Strategies for managing energy and resourceful states
- Conscious leadership and emotional intelligence skills
- An action plan to develop key management competencies

## 04. Accelerate further

**Section label:** RADAR International Program

**Title:**  
6 management modules at Executive level

**Subtitle:**  
Each module lasts 3–4 days, followed by support and mentoring (chat-bot, expert guidance). Completing all modules earns a state-accredited diploma.

**Modules:**

### Module 01

**Title:** Unlocking management potential

**Description:** Race track training (partner Kamensky Racing School) and a session on conscious management of the leader's personal and professional effectiveness.

### Module 02

**Title:** Management business modeling

**Description:** Building the leader's business model and a new company business model. Anti-crisis transformations for sustainable growth.

### Module 03

**Title:** Artificial intelligence in management

**Description:** Practical adoption of AI in making and validating management decisions.

### Module 04

**Title:** Automation and analytics

**Description:** Building an analytics system and systematic management of business processes.

### Module 05

**Title:** Product management

**Description:** Creating and improving products, enriching customer experience, growth points, hypothesis testing, CJM, product process management.

### Module 06

**Title:** Your own management project

**Description:** Repackaging your own product and business. Project defense, option for a double diploma.
